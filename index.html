VorgunTheBeta Gamer, modder, bot programmer.



VorgunTheBeta, or just Vorgun, is a Dutch-Australian programmer. He is also an avid gamer, modder of games, and even has done a few speedruns. When Vorgun isn't playing games, or modding them, he's usually working on one of his 5 discord bots, to make sure they stay up.















Neppy

Neppy is a multi use bot. She can do a lot of fancy stuff. Google search, image search, wiki stuff, and even just show some pudding.


Created on April 1st, 2016
Nep-Tunes

Nep-Tunes is a versatile music bot. Add playlists from youtube, soundcloud and more. Upvote your favourite songs.

Created on April 15th, 2016
Rom and Ram

Rom and Ram are just a simple welcome bot. Helps to make the server more welcoming.

Created on July 25th, 2016
Lúcio

Lúcio is a more upgraded version of Nep-Tunes. Best suited for an Overwatch themed server.

Created on April 10th, 2017
HanaBot

HanaBot, or just Hana, is a D.Va inspired bot. Mainly created for the D.Va Mains server, she gives roles and more. Check your Overwatch stats with her, no matter what platform you are on.

Created on June 1st, 2017
